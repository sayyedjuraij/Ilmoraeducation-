Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

Are the certificates internationally recognized?+

Yes. All certificates issued through ILMORA's partner universities are from UGC-recognized, NAAC-accredited institutions. They are accepted internationally including for UAE equivalency, overseas employment, and further studies abroad.

Do I need to handle attestation myself?+

Absolutely not. This is ILMORA's biggest differentiator. We handle 100% of the attestation, equivalency, and documentation process on your behalf. You provide us with the required documents — we do the rest.

Can I study while working full time?+

Yes — that's exactly who we built this for. All programs have weekend classes and flexible online learning options. You never need to take leave or disrupt your current job to complete your degree with ILMORA.

How affordable are the programs?+

ILMORA's programs are significantly more affordable than traditional universities. We offer flexible monthly payment plans with no hidden costs. Contact us for a free consultation and we'll share exact pricing for your program of interest.

What is UAE equivalency and do you support it?+

UAE equivalency is the process of getting your Indian degree recognized by UAE authorities, required for many professional roles and visa categories in the UAE. ILMORA specializes in this — we handle the entire process for BTech and MTech graduates.

How long does it take to complete a degree?+

Duration varies by program. UG degrees typically take 3 years, PG programs 1–2 years, and certifications can be completed in a few months. ILMORA designs intensive, focused programs to minimize duration without compromising quality.

What happens after I enroll?+

Once enrolled, you get a dedicated advisor who guides you through classes, assignments, and exams. After graduation, ILMORA handles all your post-degree documentation. You'll receive full support from day one to the day your certificate is recognized.
